# Article Catalog — franchisee.id

Scope: 96 buyer/operator education briefs across the full franchisee lifecycle. Product routes keep directory, listing, comparison, calculator, account, and commercial intents.

Domain boundaries: no profit promises, personalised investment/tax/credit advice, final legal interpretation, invented case studies, or city-name swaps. Franchisor.id may independently cover the supplier/network-owner perspective. Legal, financial, labour, privacy, safety, and sector-regulated pages require the review gates in `TOPICAL_AUTHORITY.md`.

Statistics: 16 parent topics; 96 briefs; exactly 6 briefs per topic; 12 P0, 38 P1, 37 P2, and 9 P3. Wave 1 contains 12 connected assets; later waves remain conditional on evidence and measurement.

Priority legend: `P0` first bounded cluster; `P1` high-value next decision support; `P2` supporting depth; `P3` specialist/late-lifecycle depth.
