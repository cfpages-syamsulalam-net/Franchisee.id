## Premium Add-on Decision
- Editorial article is a paid add-on by default.
- Premium can receive an article as a campaign/annual/package bonus when explicitly configured.
- Monthly editorial is acceptable only when each article has a distinct useful angle, not repeated brand-profile content.
- Recommended pricing:
  - Editorial article: Rp 750.000 one-off, or Rp 500.000 for Premium owners.
  - Editorial + social bundle: Rp 1.000.000, or Rp 750.000 for Premium owners.
  - Social media exposure: Rp 250.000 per mention, or Rp 200.000 for Premium owners.
  - Social media package: Rp 1.000.000 for 5 mentions, or Rp 750.000 for Premium owners.
  - Monthly content package: Rp 2.000.000/month, or Rp 1.500.000/month for Premium owners.
