## Guardrails
- OCR is not automatic. Proposal data is sent externally only when an admin runs the dry-run or batch action.
- Dry-run requires `OCR_KEY` and at least one enabled provider, prepares at most one proposal-image job, and processes only that job.
- OCR-derived facts remain review-only suggestions; canonical listing fields are not overwritten automatically.
